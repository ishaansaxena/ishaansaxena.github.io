About Words Work Gallery

I am Ishaan (they/them), a writer & translator based in New Delhi.

In May 2020, I graduated from Purdue University where I studied Philosophy, Mathematics, and Computer Science. I am particularly interested in topics related to Cognition, Language, and Society.

These days, I keep myself fed by writing essays and poems. I spend a lot of time working on translations between Hindi/Urdu and English. I'm also a part-time visual artist and jazz musician.

If you want to collaborate, please get in touch!
